The wellness coach needs an accountant. The accountant needs a mentor. The mentor needs a doctor. The doctor needs an architect. The architect needs a wellness coach. They're all 1 or 2 degrees from each other - and most of them don't know it yet.

This is what I'm building toward.

As I continue to watch capable, generous people creating alone - without the infrastructure that comes with traditional employment - I keep seeing the same gap. DayBalancer, Appear Network, and 2Gather are one thing: infrastructure for healthy circulation.

DayBalancer
Healthy circulation of body and mind

Helping you tap into daily sources of joy by applying your skills to serve what is needed in the moment - all guided by your values.

Appear Network
Healthy circulation of values within a small network

Helping your network show up for each other - and helping you build one if you're starting fresh. People here give and receive with people they know - skills, time, connections, care. Show up as yourself. Think a compatible matching app for your network 🌐 Everyone is accessible - compatible people find each other based on what they have and what they need. Advanced features available for those who want to do more.

2Gather
Healthy circulation of values within community

For people building communities, groups, and teams - customize your space to match compatible people for paid, volunteer, or board roles, and use AI to evaluate team fit. Team intelligence helps you build and sustain projects with the right people. Priced per build.

Money circulates here, but so does time, skill, care, and presence. Circulation comes in many forms, from different people. 700+ people are already experimenting with this new form of relationship. Come join us.
