I'm building this because I continue to watch capable, generous people creating alone without the infrastructure we're given when hired in a traditional sense. The wellness coach needs an accountant. The accountant has an idea for an app and needs a mentor. The engineering mentor needs a primary care doctor. The primary doctor needs an eco-friendly architect. The architect needs a wellness coach. Science shows that all of these people are 1 or 2 degrees from each other. This is where we help networks appear.

DayBalancer, Appear Network, and Gather are one thing: infrastructure for healthy circulation.

  • DayBalancer - healthy circulation of body and mind
  • Appear Network - healthy circulation of values within a small network
  • 2Gather - healthy circulation of values within community

DayBalancer helps you tap into daily sources of joy by applying your skills to serve what is needed in the moment - all guided by your values.

Appear helps your network show up for each other - and helps you build one if you're starting fresh. People here give and receive with people they know - skills, time, connections, care. Show up as yourself. Think a compatible matching app for your network 🌐 Everyone is accessible - compatible people find each other based on what they have and what they need. Advanced features available for those who want to do more.

For people building communities, groups, and teams, customize your space to match compatible people for paid, volunteer, or board roles, and use AI to evaluate team fit. Team intelligence helps you build and sustain projects with the right people. Priced per build.

Money circulates here, but so does time, skill, care, and presence. Circulation comes in many forms, from different people. 700+ people are already experimenting with this new form of relationship. Come join us.
